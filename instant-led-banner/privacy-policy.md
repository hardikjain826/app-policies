# Privacy Policy for Instant LED Banner

**Last Updated: April 23, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Intentional Labs** ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application, **Instant LED Banner** (the "Service").

At Intentional Labs, we believe software should be a tool, not a trap. Our apps are built on the **Principle of Least Effort**—meaning we only handle the bare minimum data required to keep your experience seamless, reliable, and ad-free.

---

## 1. The "No Noise" Promise

We do not use advertising SDKs. We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing. Your focus is yours alone.

## 2. Information Collection

**We do not collect any personal data for analytics or advertising purposes.**

The Service operates primarily locally on your device. We do not transmit, store, or share personal information, location data, or usage analytics to external servers.

## 3. Functional Data (Pro Features)

To manage your Pro status and ensure your purchases are recognized across all your devices, we use a third-party service provider to handle subscription logic. This involves the collection of:

- **A Random User ID:** A string used to identify your subscription profile.
- **Purchase History:** To verify your Monthly, Yearly, or Lifetime status.
- **Device Identifiers:** To prevent fraud and manage your access on different hardware.

This data is used strictly for **App Functionality**, is encrypted in transit, and is **never used for tracking** outside this app.

## 4. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions to function:

- **Show on Lock Screen / Wake Lock:** Used solely to keep the LED banner visible when your device is locked or idle. This is a core feature of the app.
- **Haptics/Vibration:** Used to provide tactile feedback when interacting with controls.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics or advertising services. The only third-party integration is a subscription management provider used exclusively to verify Pro status (see Section 3).

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone under the age of 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
