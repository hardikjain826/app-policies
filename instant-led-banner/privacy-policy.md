# Privacy Policy for Instant LED Banner

**Last Updated: February 17, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Hardik Jain** ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information when you use our mobile application, **Instant LED Banner** (the "Service").

## 1. Information Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

The Service operates entirely locally on your device. We do not transmit, store, or share any personal information, location data, or usage analytics to external servers.

## 2. Permissions

The app requests the following permissions to function:

*   **Show on Lock Screen / Wake Lock:** Used solely to keep the LED banner visible when your device is locked or idle. This is a core feature of the app.
*   **Haptics/Vibration:** Used to provide tactile feedback when interacting with controls.
